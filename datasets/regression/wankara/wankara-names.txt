Weather Ankara dataset

1: Description.

This file contains the wheather information of Ankara from 01/01/1994 to 28/05/1998. From given features, the goal is to predict the mean
temperature.

2: Type.			Regression
3: Origin.			Real world
4: Instances.		1609
5: Features.		10
6: Missing values.	No

7: Header.

@relation wankara
@attribute Max_temperature real [23.0, 100.0]
@attribute Min_temperature real [-7.1, 65.5]
@attribute Dewpoint real [-3.1, 57.6]
@attribute Precipitation real [0.0, 4.0]
@attribute Sea_level_pressure real [29.46, 30.6]
@attribute Standard_pressure real [26.3, 27.18]
@attribute Visibility real [0.2, 11.5]
@attribute Wind_speed real [0.0, 18.0]
@attribute Max_wind_speed real [2.19, 57.4]
@attribute Mean_temperature real [7.9, 81.8]
@inputs Max_temperature, Min_temperature, Dewpoint, Precipitation, Sea_level_pressure, Standard_pressure, Visibility, Wind_speed, Max_wind_speed
@outputs Mean_temperature
